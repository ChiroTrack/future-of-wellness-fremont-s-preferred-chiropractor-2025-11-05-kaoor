About LIT Chiropractic

Founded in 2015, LIT Chiropractic began with a simple vision: to revolutionize how Fremont residents approach their health and wellness. Our founder, Dr. Michelle Chen, recognized a gap in the market for truly patient-centered chiropractic care that combined modern technology with compassionate, personalized treatment. What started as a single clinic has grown into a trusted wellness destination, serving thousands of satisfied patients and building a reputation as Fremont's preferred chiropractic provider.

Today, our mission remains unwavering: to empower individuals to achieve optimal spinal health and overall wellness through evidence-based chiropractic care, cutting-edge technology, and comprehensive patient education. We believe that true health comes from addressing the root causes of pain and dysfunction, not just treating symptoms. Our core values—integrity, excellence, compassion, and innovation—guide every interaction with our patients. We're committed to creating a welcoming, professional environment where every person feels heard, respected, and empowered to take control of their health journey.

During your first visit, you'll start with a comprehensive consultation where we learn about your health history, current symptoms, and wellness goals. We'll perform a thorough physical examination and may take digital X-rays to assess your spinal health. Based on our findings, we'll create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs. This initial appointment typically takes 45-60 minutes, and we'll explain everything in detail so you understand your condition and treatment options.

The number of treatments varies depending on your condition, severity of symptoms, and overall health. Acute conditions may require 4-6 weeks of treatment, while chronic conditions might require longer-term care. During your consultation, we'll provide an estimated treatment plan and timeline. Most patients begin to notice improvement within the first 2-3 weeks. We continuously evaluate your progress and adjust your treatment plan as needed to ensure optimal results.
